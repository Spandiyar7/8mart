# Brand & lifestyle photos

Lifestyle / atmosphere photos of the 8MART studio, florists at work,
the warehouse and happy customers. Used in editorial frames on the
About page, the Instagram wall and the final call-to-action.

## How to switch on

1. Add the files below to this folder.
2. Open `src/config/media.ts` and set `brandPhotosAvailable = true`.

## Recommended files

```
studio.jpg        the workshop / warehouse              (4:5 portrait)
florist.jpg       a florist assembling a bouquet         (4:5 portrait)
delivery.jpg      a courier / a delivered bouquet        (4:3 landscape)
insta-1.jpg … insta-6.jpg   square shots for the Instagram wall (1:1)
cta-bouquet.jpg   a hero bouquet for the final CTA       (4:5 portrait)
```

## Recommended specs

- **Size:** ~1400 px on the long edge.
- **Format:** JPG or WebP, under ~300 KB each.
- Until added, frames show premium generated placeholders.
